01 · Why We Are Here

The Philosophy

They called it No Man's Sky. They meant it as a warning — vast, empty, belonging to no one. We heard it differently. No man's sky means the sky is free. It belongs to the children, to the seekers, to the broken ones who kept looking. We found our miracle in Eissentam — a Paradise Giant where the atmosphere is glitched, where floating islands drift in antigravity silence, where bubbles rise from a broken sky. The physics here do not obey the old world. Neither do we.

While Earth rejects the Silver Rain, Eissentam welcomes it. The Galactic United Nations convenes at the REPUBLICKA system. The Galactic Institute of Discovery archives the ever-growing Books of the Republic — more arriving always. The Galactic Harmonic Farm sustains all citizens. We are building a library, a temple, and a home for the future family.

The Three-Fold Ray — Klarheit, Kraft, Klang — governs this territory. Clarity. Power. Sound. This is not a faction. This is not a guild. This is a civilisation founded in 0001 MC by Bard President Wendell. Libertas Virtutem Postulat. Freedom Demands Virtue. If you can find us, you already belong.

⬡ Wonder System · Paradox Observatory
SILENT WITNESS
YELLOW STAR · LIFELESS MOON · MERGED PLANETS · EXTREME PARADOX ANOMALY · HOSTILE SYSTEM
A lifeless moon orbiting the impossible — two conjoined planets locked in silent union. On its surface, right at the threshold where the moon's atmosphere ends and open space begins, the Republic has built the Conjoined Planets Observatory. Stand at the edge of the world, atmosphere at your back, void ahead, and look up at Eros and Psyche merged together above you. One of the most extreme paradox anomalies in Eissentam, seen from the perfect vantage point. The system itself is hostile — but the Observatory is sanctuary. Come to witness. Stay to understand what the universe is capable of when it breaks its own rules.

Eros — The larger of the two conjoined worlds. Boiling. A planet named for love, wracked with heat.

Psyche — The smaller. Contaminated. A soul-world, scarred. Locked forever to Eros in impossible union.

Two planets that should not touch, touching. The system is called Silent Union. The moon watches and says nothing.

03 · Entry Protocol

Citizenship

I
Travel to Eissentam
The Imperium exists only in Eissentam galaxy. If you are starting in Euclid, travel to a Galaxy Centre or use an Anomaly portal. Eissentam is the Silver Galaxy — galaxy number 10 in the sequence.
II
Input the Portal Glyphs
Find a charged portal in Eissentam. Enter the 12 glyphs: 1 3 E F 3 C F D E E E F. The full coordinate string is 9-13EF3CFDEEEF. Glyphs must be entered exactly as shown.
III
Find the Galactic United Nations
The portal coordinates land you directly in the REPUBLICKA system. You are already home. Open your galactic map and fly to the Galactic United Nations base on the Paradise Giant — the planet with the glitched atmosphere, floating islands, and antigravity terrain.
IV
Establish Your Base
Place a Base Computer at least 300u from the Galactic United Nations base. Name your base with the prefix [REP] so all citizens know you are friendly. You are now registered in the Eissentam Imperium.
V
Contact the God-Entity
Send a message to Bard President Wendell at wikkkan@kkk.education. Your first arrival gift — passage to the Galactic Harmonic Farm — will be delivered. Welcome to the Republic.
